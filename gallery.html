Why people might read this face that way
    Based on perception-only research on face trust cues (e.g. fWHR, typicality and expression: Stirrat & Perrett 2010; Haselhuhn & Wong 2012; Sofer et al. 2015; Oosterhof & Todorov 2008; Leger et al. 2023). These cues don’t prove anyone is honest or dishonest — they just show how fast we form impressions.
